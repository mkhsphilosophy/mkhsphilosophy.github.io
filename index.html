MKHS Philosophy Club

The Philosophy Club's goal is to expose students of Mark Keppel High School to philosophical literature and foster an open academic environment to analyze and discuss a diverse array of ideas.

This website will serve as a hub of information for those interested in learning more about the philosophy club. All information regarding meeting times and upcoming events may be sought out on the official Philosophy Club Facebook page.
